What is Dicey Dungeon?

Dicey Dungeon is a digital dungeon master's companion that brings the art of procedural dungeon generation to your fingertips. By harnessing the chaos of a full set of polyhedral dice (D4 through D100), this tool weaves together room layouts, encounters, and narrative elements to create unique dungeon experiences.

Each roll of the dice shapes your dungeon's story - from winding hallways and hidden chambers to the ancient mysteries and dangerous encounters that await within. The integrated visualization system brings each room to life, providing clear, tactical layouts that work seamlessly with standard 5-foot grid measurements used in most tabletop RPGs.

Whether you're an experienced dungeon master seeking inspiration for your next adventure, or a storyteller looking to let the dice guide your tale, Dicey Dungeon offers endless possibilities for crafting memorable dungeon delves.

Special Rules

  • Matching numbers on D20, D8, or D12 trigger additional encounter rolls
  • All three matching triggers triple encounters
  • Exit placement follows clockwise order from entrance
  • Room visualizations update automatically with each roll
  • Rooms can be saved individually or as part of a sequence
